EpicLand Theme Park

EpicLand Theme park is full of different fun and adventurous rides to explore. From being high up in the air flying on the higher rides to being lower on the ground exploring.

EpicLand Theme Park was founded in 1990 in Queenstown, New Zealand. In 2000 the business took off with thousands of tourists arriving and exploring the park everyday. With the park being close to the city center there is so much to explore and enjoy.
